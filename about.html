About EduEnhance

Empowering Every Student — Everywhere

EduEnhance is a free online learning initiative built to ensure *equal learning opportunities* for every student across India — especially those from under-resourced backgrounds. Our platform provides *free, high-quality educational content* for JEE, NEET, and CBSE (Classes 9–12), with *no login, no subscriptions, and no ads*.

With growing smartphone access in rural and urban areas alike, EduEnhance removes digital barriers and offers:

  • 📘 Handwritten and typed notes (PDFs)
  • 📚 NCERT books and toppers' strategies
  • 📄 Previous year papers (2020–2024)
  • 🎥 Interview videos — embedded directly
